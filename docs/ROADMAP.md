# Spareflow AI - Platform Roadmap & Future Directions

This document specifies the feature progression roadmap, architectural milestones, and community-driven expansion plans for **Spareflow AI**.

---

## Phase 1: Enterprise Isolation Foundation (Completed)
- [x] Multi-Tenant database schema layout.
- [x] Strict user JWT access tokens and role-based access controls (RBAC).
- [x] Modular codebase architecture (Separation of Services & Repositories).
- [x] Secure AES-256-CBC field encryption for customer contacts and sensitive identifiers.
- [x] Interactive dev testing playpen / UI simulation platform.

---

## Phase 2: Inventory & Ledger Hardening (In Progress)
- [ ] Comprehensive multi-warehouse inventory level tracking and replenishment algorithms.
- [ ] Automatic reorder point triggering with webhook payloads to vendors.
- [ ] Immutable stock ledger and movement audits on all transfer nodes.
- [ ] Barcode scanning support via mobile devices for faster warehouse intake.

---

## Phase 3: Financials & Zoho Integration (Planned)
- [ ] Full accounting ledger integrations (Accounts Receivable / Accounts Payable).
- [ ] Native integration bridges for syncing contacts and sales logs with Zoho Inventory, Quickbooks, and Tally ERP.
- [ ] Customizable professional PDF invoice template rendering.
- [ ] Customer portal enabling secure credit payments, tracking statements, and return authorizations.

---

## Phase 4: AI Optimization & Predictive Analytics (Visionary)
- [ ] **AI-Powered Demand Forecasting**: Analyze historical sales of automobile spare parts to predict seasonal changes and avoid low-stock or over-stock states.
- [ ] **Automated Parts Parsing**: Parse raw invoices, bill of materials, or parts manifests using Gemini Vision and extract structured entities (SKU, quantities, manufacturers).
- [ ] **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: Algorithmic adjustment of parts sales prices based on demand velocity, supply scarcity, and vendor procurement margins.
